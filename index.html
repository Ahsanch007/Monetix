How Monetix.AI
solution works

Automated Real-Time Content Analysis

Monetix.AI analyze the content on your website using state-of-the-art data science techniques. Through contextual recognition, we gain a deep understanding of the topics, themes, and context present in your website content.

Interest Parameter Assignment

AI-algorithms categorize and classify your website's content by assigning a set of interest parameters to each webpage. These parameters cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to fashion, news, etc.

Precise Ad Matching with AI

When purchasing ad space, we harness the power of AI to match specific parameters required by ad platforms like Google with those we've identified in your content. This precise mat-ching ensures that ads align perfectly with each webpage's context, delivering accurate and high-quality ad displays to your audience.

GDPR-Compliant Approach

Monetix.AI takes privacy seriously. We do not handle user data; instead, we work exclusively with the platform's requests, making us fully compliant with GDPR regulations. Our focus is on enhancing the ad experience based on content, ensuring the privacy and data security of your audience.
